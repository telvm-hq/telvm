## Purpose

These documents describe **informal hobby education** for adults and supervised participants. They are **not** engineering sign-off, **not** roadworthiness guidance, and **not** a warranty that any build is safe for any person or place.

## Use of space and vehicles

- Operate only where **you have permission** and where **pedestrians and traffic are not at risk**.
- **Electric longboards and RC vehicles** can cause injury; **helmets and closed-toe shoes** are recommended for anyone near active drive tests.
- **Public roads** and **sidewalks** may be **illegal or inappropriate** for experimental vehicles depending on jurisdiction; hosts must confirm local rules.

## Batteries

Mishandled rechargeable batteries can **ignite** or **vent**. Participants accept responsibility for following [safety-and-batteries.md](safety-and-batteries.md) and manufacturer instructions for chargers and packs.

## Pawn-shop and donor parts

Salvaged electronics can be **faulty**, **mislabeled**, or **unsafe**. Verify ratings, inspect insulation, and discard suspicious packs.

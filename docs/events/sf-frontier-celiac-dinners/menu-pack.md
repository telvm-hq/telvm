# Menu pack — nine sessions (30 covers, strict GF, carnivore/keto-forward)

**Yield**: all recipes scale to **30 guests** unless noted.  
**Service model**: **Cold/finger** first → **one oven wave** (protein + veg on sheet pans) → **cold dessert**.  
**Series calendar**: **Tuesday, April 28, 2026** through **Tuesday, June 23, 2026** (weekly). Session-to-date mapping: [schedule.md](schedule.md).  
**Rule**: verify **every** packaged item (spices, mayo, mustard, vinegar, charcuterie, dairy, chocolate) against **supplier allergen statements** before ordering.

---

## Shared prep calendar (every session)

| When | Tasks |
|------|--------|
| **T-48 h** | Finalize counts; confirm GF labels on all deliveries; print menu cards. |
| **T-24 h** | Break down proteins; marinate if applicable (no soy/malt); wash and par-prep veg; make cold sauces; set up labeled GF-only lowboy. |
| **T-4 h** | Plate cold apps in pass coolers; set 30 labeled covers; brief staff on hidden gluten. |
| **T-90 min** | Fire oven to session target temp; stage sheet pans. |

## Session 1 — Herb chicken + broccolini

### Cold / finger

1. **Crudités + ranch** — GF ranch (verify packet or house: buttermilk, mayo labeled GF, herbs, lemon; no malt vinegar). **6 lb** mixed veg sticks (carrot, celery, cucumber, radish).
2. **Prosciutto-wrapped melon** — 60 half-bites: **2 cantaloupe**, **30 thin prosciutto slices** (verify GF curing), toothpicks.
3. **Deviled eggs** — **5 dozen eggs** hard-boiled → 30 halves displayed + extras; mayo/mustard GF.

### Hot (single pull)

- **Chicken quarters (bone-in, skin-on)** — **~22–25 lb** raw (30 pieces ~12–14 oz each before cook; adjust to supplier cut).  
  Rub: olive oil, kosher salt, pepper, chopped rosemary/thyme (dried herbs verify GF).  
  **425 °F convection** until **165 °F** thigh internal minimum; rest before quartering if large pieces.
- **Broccolini** — **4–5 lb**; toss with olive oil, garlic slices, lemon zest, salt; same oven, **425 °F** ~12–18 min after chicken is **~10 min from done** (stagger so both finish together).

**GF verification**: dried herbs, mustard, mayo, ranch mix.

### Dessert

- **Dark chocolate pots** — GF dark chocolate, cream, egg yolks, tiny salt; **30 small jars**; make **day before**.

## Session 2 — Pork shoulder steaks + mushrooms

### Cold / finger

1. **Shrimp cocktail** — **6–7 lb** cooked 16–20 ct shrimp; cocktail sauce: ketchup, horseradish, lemon, **GF Worcestershire or omit** (many contain gluten).
2. **Salami roses** — GF-labeled salami only; **~2.5 lb** total; toothpicks.
3. **Cucumber rounds + smoked salmon schmear** — English cucumbers; cream cheese; smoked salmon; dill; verify **smoked salmon** GF.

### Hot

- **Pork shoulder steaks / “country ribs”** — **~25–28 lb** bone-in or boneless steaks, **~1 inch** thick for even sheet-pan cook. Dry rub: salt, pepper, smoked paprika (verify), cumin. **400 °F** to **145 °F** internal (hold policy per your HACCP; many serve **145 °F** rested for quality—confirm with counsel/health dept expectations).
- **Roasted mushrooms** — **5 lb** cremini, olive oil, thyme, salt, **400 °F** same window.

### Dessert

- **Vanilla panna cotta + berry compote** — gelatin or agar per recipe; berries cooked with sugar + lemon; **day before**.

## Session 3 — Tri-tip + asparagus

### Cold / finger

1. **Caprese skewers** — cherry tomatoes, ciliegine mozzarella, basil; **balsamic reduction** from **single-ingredient balsamic** or verified GF glaze.
2. **Olives** — brined; check **stuffed** varieties for wheat.
3. **Marinated artichokes** — jar label **GF**; drain well.

### Hot

- **Tri-tip** — **1 whole tri-tip ~4.5–6 lb** is tight for 30 small slices; prefer **2 tri-tips** or **add beef skewers** for portion security. Salt/pepper, light coffee/chili rub if desired (verify chili blend). **Sear + 375 °F** roast to **125–130 °F** for medium-rare center, slice thin across grain.
- **Asparagus** — **5 bunches**; oil, salt, lemon finish post-oven.

### Dessert

- **Salted caramel mousse cups** — use **GF caramel** (no malt); gelatin-stabilized mousse; **day before**.

## Session 4 — Lamb leg steaks + Brussels

### Cold / finger

1. **Chicken liver mousse** — livers, butter, shallots, brandy (verify GF), thyme; pass with **veg sticks** and **GF cornichons**.
2. **Capicola bundles** — verify GF; wrap around **pickle spears** or cheese sticks.
3. **Pickle board** — verify liquids/spices GF.

### Hot

- **Lamb leg steaks** — **~22–25 lb** bone-in steaks if available; otherwise **boneless leg** cut to **6 oz** portions. Rub: rosemary, garlic, olive oil, salt. **425 °F** to **135–145 °F** depending on desired doneness; rest.
- **Brussels sprouts** — **5 lb** halved, oil, salt; finish with **GF Dijon** vinaigrette tableside if desired (verify mustard).

### Dessert

- **Lemon posset** — cream, sugar, lemon juice; **day before**; **no biscuit** or use **almond meal crisp** if you add crunch (verify almond handling).

## Session 5 — Salmon side + zucchini

### Cold / finger

1. **Tuna tartare spoons** — sushi-grade tuna **~4 lb**; **GF tamari**; sesame oil; scallion; serve on **endive leaves** or cucumber boats (no soy sauce).
2. **Bacon-wrapped dates** — **60 pieces**; no flour; secure with toothpick; **partially bake earlier**, finish timing carefully or serve room-temp (safer for synchronized service).
3. **Cheese board** — hard cheeses + nuts; **read blue cheese labels**.

### Hot

- **Salmon fillet (one or two sides)** — **~8–10 lb** skin-on total for **4–5 oz** portions; dill, lemon slices, olive oil, salt. **375 °F** to **120–125 °F** center unless policy requires higher; **do not overcook** for batch—practice timing.
- **Zucchini** — **6 lb** half-moons, garlic oil, **high heat last 12 min**.

### Dessert

- **Chocolate mousse** — GF chocolate, pasteurized egg or gelatin method per your food code comfort.

## Session 6 — Chicken thighs + loaded cauliflower

### Cold / finger

1. **Antipasto** — mortadella (verify), mozzarella rolls, **giardiniera** (jar GF), marinated peppers, nuts.
2. **Optional**: marinated olives repeat from session 3 if cost-saving.

### Hot

- **Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs** — **~25–28 lb** (~1 piece per guest + a few extra). **425 °F** skin-up, crisp; **165 °F** minimum.
- **Loaded cauliflower** — **4 large heads** broken into florets, roast with oil; finish with shredded cheddar and **pre-cooked bacon crumbles** (verify GF cure); melt in oven last **5 min** with chicken.

### Dessert

- **Panna cotta + citrus** — supremed citrus or compote; GF.

## Session 7 — Prime rib + carrots

### Cold / finger

1. **Steak tartare** — **only if** local code and insurer allow; otherwise substitute **beef carpaccio** with arugula. **GF “cracker”**: **parmesan crisps** (baked piles of shredded parm).
2. **Shrimp salad in endive** — cooked shrimp, celery, lemon, mayo (GF), chive.
3. **Cheese crisps** on side for crunch.

### Hot

- **Prime rib / standing rib roast** — **~18–22 lb** bone-in roast typical for **30 × ~7–8 oz** after trim and loss—**confirm with butcher yield**. **Low-start or reverse sear** to hit **one** pull time; **125–130 °F** pre-rest for medium-rare band.
- **Horseradish cream** — sour cream, **horseradish** (verify no wheat), lemon, salt.
- **Roasted carrots** — **6 lb** baby carrots or coins, honey optional, oil, salt.

### Dessert

- **Coffee cream + berries** — cold **coffee-infused** custard or whipped mascarpine cream; keep **decaf option** labeled.

## Session 8 — Pork tenderloin + green beans

### Cold / finger

1. **Smoked trout dip** — **2 lb** smoked trout, cream cheese, lemon, chives; celery sticks.
2. **Salami chips** — thin salami baked flat (verify GF) **day-of early**.
3. **Pickles** — as session 4.

### Hot

- **Pork tenderloin** — **2–3 pieces** (~6–8 lb total) for **30 × ~3 oz** slices; **seam out** silver skin; sear; **400 °F** to **145 °F**; rest; slice on bias.
- **Green beans** — **5 lb** with olive oil + **shallots** (or almonds if no allergy).

### Dessert

- **Coconut cream chia pudding** — chia, coconut milk, vanilla, **low sugar**; berries on top.

## Session 9 — Finale (charcuterie + branzino **or** chuck roast)

Pick **one** hot centerpiece based on kitchen skill.

### Cold / finger

- **GF charcuterie tower**: meats verified GF, **cornichons**, mustard (verify), **cheese**, nuts, **crudités**, deviled eggs (**5 dozen** again).

### Hot option A — Whole branzino

- **15 whole fish** (half fish per guest) or **30 small** if available—logistics heavy; scales and timing difficult. **Only** if kitchen has **fish oven discipline** and extra hands. **425 °F** with lemon/herb; **145 °F** flesh.

### Hot option B — Beef chuck roast (recommended for batch)

- **2 large chuck roasts** (~15–18 lb total) braised earlier, **then** optional **flash roast** for crust service—**or** serve as fork-tender slices from holding (not strictly “from oven” but honest for 30 covers). If you need a true oven pull, use **portioned chuck flap / Denver steaks** on sheet pans instead.

### Veg

- **Roasted fennel** — **6–8 bulbs** wedged, oil, salt, **same pull** as beef option.

### Dessert

- **Berries + mascarpone cream** — macerated berries; sweetened mascarpone/whip mix.
